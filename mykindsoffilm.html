My Kinds of Film

Welcome to my film page! Here I share a selection of films and TV shows that captivate my imagination. From thought-provoking trailers to iconic cinematic masterpieces, explore my curated collection and discover why these films and series resonate with me.

TV SHOWS
Television Series that Inspire

Incognito

Incognito is a Philippine action drama television series produced by Starr Creatives and Studio Three Sixty. Directed by Lester Pimentel Ong, it stars Richard Gutierrez, Baron Geisler, Maris Racal, and others. It is scheduled to premiere on January 20, 2025.

When Life Gives You Tangerines

When Life Gives You Tangerines is a South Korean romantic slice-of-life television series written by Lim Sang-choon, directed by Kim Won-seok, and starring IU, Park Bo-gum, Moon So-ri, and Park Hae-joon. It premiered on Netflix on March 7, 2025.

MOVIES
Blockbuster Films and Cinematic Masterpieces

Spider-Man: No Way Home

Spider-Man: No Way Home is a 2021 American superhero film featuring Tom Holland as Peter Parker. The film explores the multiverse with returning villains and allies, creating a thrilling crossover event.

Avengers: Endgame

Avengers: Endgame is a 2019 American superhero film that concludes the Infinity Saga. Directed by the Russo Brothers, it unites the surviving Avengers for one final battle against Thanos.
